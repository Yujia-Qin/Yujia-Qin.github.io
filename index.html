About Me

Career Interest

I am a computer science major with an interest in art and design. I love coding for a good cause, for exciting projects and research that enhance people's lives. I find these two disciplines are fundamentally complimentary -- computer science is built upon beautifully designed systems, while many design principles are quite logical.

Artificial inteligence, machine learning, media computing, and many other unknown computer disciplines are all quite attractive to me. I am exploring my "destiny" in computer science, just as Hesse did in the book Damian:

to search for yourself, become sure of yourself, feel your way toward along your own path, wherever it led.
